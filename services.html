HNG company the black stone in road projects, building projects is drilled and blasted with a warrenty. The black stones in the quarries are also drilled and blasted.

DRILLING

Rock on Ground runs a fleet of Sandvik top hammer drill rigs DP1500i, DP1500 and DP1100 to provide quality small hole diameter drilling for 89mm to 140mm diameter blastholes. The DP1500i has successfully drilled 140mm blastholes at numerous hard rock open pits. Rock on Ground provides larger DTH drilling with its fleet of Atlas Copco D65 drill rigs that can drill 115mm to 203mm blastholes. Our drill crews are professional drillers, trained to operate safely and efficiently. They are experienced in all facets of blasthole drilling including collar piping in wet hard pits and presplit drilling.

EXPLOSIVE SUPPLY

Sourcing explosives through supply agreements with major explosive suppliers, Rock on Ground has the added benefit of owning and operating a fleet of MMU/MPU trucks to deliver ANFO and emulsion products down the hole. Our project teams are experienced at establishing and operating major bulk storage and reload facilities for both major mining, and short-term civil projects.
