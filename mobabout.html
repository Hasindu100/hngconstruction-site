ABOUT US

HNG CONSTRACTORS is an establishment registered in SriLanka in the year 2010 and has been in business up to this date in the field of drilling and blasting of rocks by the use of chemicals and explosives.

This firm of ours work for and jointly with major construction companies.

All sub contracts and assignments undertaken by us so far have been completed very satisfactorily and excellently.

All the construction companies that have given us sub contracts have praised the high quality standars and efficiency of our work.

We have among our personnel very skilled workmen,technicians,foremen,supervisors,who comprise a team of highly qualified persons working under a senior miningEngineer.

The owner of our firm is Mr.J.H.Gunathunga who is a goverment certified technical officer specialized in industrial blasting works.
