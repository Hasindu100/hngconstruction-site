DRILLING

Rock on Ground provides larger DTH drilling with its fleet of Atlas Copco D65 drill rigs that can drill 115mm to 203mm blastholes.

Our drill crews are professional drillers, trained to operate safely and efficiently. They are experienced in all facets of blasthole drilling including collar piping in wet hard pits and presplit drilling.

EXPLOSIVES SUPPLY

Sourcing explosives through supply agreements with major explosive suppliers, Rock on Ground has the added benefit of owning and operating a fleet of MMU/MPU trucks to deliver ANFO and emulsion products down the hole.
